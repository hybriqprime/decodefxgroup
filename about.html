My journey into forex began not in a trading room, but in frustration. After years of consuming free content, blowing two accounts, and questioning whether consistent profitability was even real — I committed to understanding the market the way institutional players do.

The turning point came when I discovered that retail traders are systematically being used as liquidity by the banks. Understanding Smart Money Concepts changed everything. For the first time, price action made sense. Not because of indicators or signals I was copying — but because I understood why price moved.

Over the next three years, I refined my edge across dozens of currency pairs, commodities, and market conditions. I documented everything. Built systems. Developed a risk framework that has kept me in profit through volatile markets, news events, and sideways conditions.

DecodeFXGroup was born from a simple belief: the knowledge that transforms traders should not be gatekept. Every student who joins gets the same framework that took me years to build — structured, tested, and taught with brutal honesty.

This is not a get-rich-quick academy. This is a professional school for serious traders. If you're ready to treat trading as a business, I'm ready to teach you how.
