Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DecodeFXGroup.

What exactly do I get for the $300 enrollment fee?

Your $300 enrollment grants lifetime access to the full 12-module LMS curriculum, access to the private student community, the risk management playbook, and a certificate of completion on finishing all modules. This is a one-time fee, no recurring charge for the course itself.

After your complimentary 30 days, you can choose to continue with our Standard Signals plan at $50/month or upgrade to VIP Elite at $150/month. Both plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Your course access remains permanent regardless of whether you subscribe to signals.

Do I need prior trading experience to join?

No prior experience is required for the Academy enrollment. Module 1 starts with absolute fundamentals, currency pairs, how the market works, session times. However, we recommend a minimum commitment to learning. This is not a passive income scheme; it requires dedicated study and practice. If you've never heard of a pip, you'll be fine. If you're unwilling to study, this isn't for you.

How are signals delivered and what do they include?

All signals are delivered via our private Telegram channel. Every signal includes: the currency pair or instrument, trade direction (Buy/Sell), exact entry price or zone, stop loss level, and take profit targets (TP1, TP2, TP3). We also provide the confluence reasoning so you understand why the trade was taken you're learning, not just copying.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept two methods: USDT cryptocurrency via TRC20 wallet , and direct bank transfer to our Access Bank account. For Naira payments, the exchange rate applied is ₦1,600 per dollar. After crypto or bank transfer payments, send your proof to our WhatsApp for immediate activation.

Is there a refund policy?

Due to the nature of digital educational products, we do not offer refunds once course access has been granted. We encourage you to review our free Telegram content, ask us questions on WhatsApp, and read our testimonials before enrolling. We're confident in the quality of the product, but want you to be confident too before purchasing.

Do I need a funded trading account to enroll?

No. You can enroll in the Academy and complete all 12 modules in demo or paper trading mode. We actually recommend completing at least the first 4 modules before funding a live account. For the managed trading service. You provide only the investor (read-only) login, we never have withdrawal access.

What's the difference between Standard and VIP Elite signals?

Standard ($50/mo) covers forex pairs, gold, and indices with 2–4 signals per day, weekly market analysis, and community access. VIP Elite ($150/mo) includes everything in Standard plus: crypto signals, extended gold coverage, a monthly 1-on-1 review session with Nathan, priority signal delivery before Standard members, and access to a smaller exclusive VIP group with higher-detail analysis.

Can I cancel my signal subscription anytime?

Yes. Signal subscriptions are month-to-month. Simply message us on WhatsApp before your renewal date and we'll cancel without question. Your course access remains permanent after cancellation, that's yours for life. We'd rather you come back later than feel trapped in a subscription.
