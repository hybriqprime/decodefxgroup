FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining. If you still have questions, WhatsApp us.

What do I get for the $300 enrollment fee? +
You get access to the full DecodeFXGroup LMS (12 in-depth modules), immediate access to the private Telegram channel, and 30 days of VIP live signals with entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels. No recurring fee for the course.
What happens after the 30 free signal days end? +
After your complimentary 30 days, you can continue receiving live signals by subscribing to either our Standard plan ($50/month) or our VIP Elite plan ($150/month). Your course access remains completely unaffected, you keep that for life.
Do I need trading experience to join? +
No. The curriculum starts from absolute zero. What forex is, how currency pairs work, and how to set up a trading platform. Whether you're a complete beginner or an intermediate trader wanting to upgrade your edge, the programme covers you.
How are signals delivered? +
All signals are sent directly to our private Telegram channel in real-time, formatted with the currency pair, direction (BUY/SELL), entry price, stop-loss, and take-profit levels. Modules 11 and 12 in the LMS teach you exactly how to read and execute them correctly.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept payments via Paystack (card, bank transfer, USSD-Naira) and Binance/USDT (crypto). After payment, send your receipt to our WhatsApp (+234 817 313 3453) or Telegram (@Nathanpips_bot) for manual account activation within 30 minutes.
Is there a refund policy? +
Due to the digital nature of the course and immediate access to signal intelligence, all sales are final. We strongly recommend reviewing all public content, the about page, curriculum overview, and testimonials before enrolling. If you have any questions before purchasing, WhatsApp us first.
Do I need a funded trading account to benefit? +
No. You can practice on a demo account while learning. Many students use the first 2–3 months on demo to master execution and risk management before funding a live account. We teach you to be consistently profitable on demo first funding comes after the skill is proven.
What is the difference between Standard and VIP signals? +
Standard signals ($50/month) cover major forex pairs with daily entries, SL, and TP. VIP Elite ($150/month) adds cryptocurrency pairs, commodities (gold, oil), priority delivery, trade management updates, and a monthly one-on-one trade review session with the lead analyst.
Can I cancel my signals subscription anytime? +
Yes. Signal subscriptions are month-to-month. Simply do not renew when your billing period ends, and your signal access will stop at the end of that month. Your course access (the $300 enrollment) is never affected.
